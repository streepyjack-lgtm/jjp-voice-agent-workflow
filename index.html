Phase 01 Inbound & Intake
📞
01
Inbound Call Received
Trigger

A customer dials the business line. The AI voice agent instantly answers — no hold music, no wait queues. The session is initialized and the call is ready for processing.

Handoff to AI
🗣️
02
Greet & Collect Customer Info
Conversation

The agent greets the caller naturally and gathers essential details — name, phone number, email, and reason for the appointment — through guided conversational prompts.

Info Confirmed
Phase 02 Scheduling & Booking
📅
03
Check Calendar Availability
Live Lookup

The agent queries the business calendar in real time, applying scheduling rules, buffer times, and staff availability to surface open slots for the caller to choose from.

Slots Available
✅
04
Book the Appointment
Confirmed

The caller selects a time, or the agent recommends the best available slot. The appointment is written to the calendar, the slot is locked, and the booking is recorded in the system.

Record Update
📝
05
Update Appointment & Customer Records
Data Sync

The agent automatically syncs all confirmed details — customer name, contact info, appointment date, time, and service type — to the CRM and calendar system, ensuring records are accurate and up to date before any notifications are sent.

Booking Locked
Phase 03 Notifications & Follow-Up
💬
06
Send Confirmation Message
Instant Send

The moment the appointment is booked, a confirmation is sent to the customer via SMS, email, or both — including appointment details, location or link, and a calendar invite.

Reminder Queued
⏰
07
Send Pre-Appointment Reminder
Automated

A reminder fires automatically — typically 24 hours and 1 hour before the appointment — with no manual input required. Each reminder includes an option to reschedule or cancel.

Flow Complete
🎯
08
Appointment Confirmed — Session Closed
Complete

The booking flow concludes successfully. The interaction is logged and the AI agent immediately becomes available to handle the next incoming call.
